Role Descriptions

Alliance Marshal
  • Lead the alliance and coordinate overall structure
  • Support the R4 team in decision-making
  • Manage diplomatic relations with other alliances
  • Oversee the PlayerManager system
Operations Commander – VS
  • Announce “Save” or “All-in” each Sunday
  • Share VS tips and opponent insights
  • Track player points throughout the week
  • Encourage active participation
  • Remind players privately if needed
Tactical Events Officer
  • Activate the Marshal event and position the Marshal
  • Start the Marshal event at the planned time
  • Plan and prepare the Zombie Siege
  • Set event levels for both Marshal and Zombie
  • Write and post announcements with day and time
Commander – Desert Division
  • Activate and create both teams on Monday
  • Define strategy and roles for each team
  • Motivate players to join and communicate expectations
  • Write a post-event summary if needed
Personnel Officer
  • Search for new players when space is available
  • Provide regular updates on contacted or interested recruits
  • Welcome new players with a friendly message
  • Remove inactive members with a respectful message
  • Inform the alliance of departures and their reasons, if possible
Intel & Strategy Commander
  • Share useful game tips in alliance messages and Discord
  • Provide updates on game changes and patch notes
  • Inform the team about new seasons and event changes
  • Answer player questions and guide them with useful info
Logistics Officer – Train Unit
  • Inform the train leader in the morning and assign the VIP guest
  • Notify the VIP player of their seat for the day
  • Plan the train leader schedule from Sunday to Tuesday
  • Assign and announce the daily train
Supply Officer – Donations
  • Check daily if players are contributing to alliance donations
  • Politely remind those who missed their donations
  • Report on Sunday any players who didn’t donate all week
Special Operations Officer
  • Plan special events like Glacieradon and General’s Trial
  • Research and explain new event types to the team
  • Inform players early about upcoming events
  • Announce events clearly with date and time
  • Lead and launch the events as scheduled
Discipline & Conduct Officer
  • Ensure no player is blamed, shamed, or insulted publicly
  • Step in during chat conflicts or toxic behavior
  • Prevent players from instructing others inappropriately
  • Clarify that only R4 or assigned roles give official feedback
Commander – War Theater
  • Plan war events such as Capitol and Season Wars
  • Ensure personal presence or appoint a reliable substitute
  • Motivate players to participate and show up
